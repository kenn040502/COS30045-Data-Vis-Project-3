How to interpret this visualisation:

This chart combines a radar plot and a choropleth map to show how roadside drug testing varies across different locations.

- The radar chart displays each jurisdiction's total number of tests, allowing you to compare the overall scale of enforcement activity between states.
- The map highlights the distribution of testing volume across Australia. Darker shades represent higher amounts of testing, while greyed-out states indicate locations with no recorded tests in the selected area.

Use the dropdown menu above to focus on a specific testing location. When a location is selected, the radar chart highlights its point of comparison, while the choropleth updates to show its contribution to different states.

Hovering over map regions reveals more detailed statistics, including the percentage contribution and the positive-test rate for that state.
