Project overview

As part of our university degree project, we present the Australian Police Drug Test Dashboard - a D3.js visualisation of roadside drug testing outcomes across Australia. We built this site to turn BITRE enforcement data into clear, interactive insights that support discussion on trends and road safety.

  • Course: COS30045 Data Visualisation, Swinburne University of Technology.
  • Dataset: BITRE Roadside Enforcement (drug tests) 2008-2024.
  • Stack: D3.js with semantic HTML/CSS.
  • Purpose: Make enforcement trends and outcomes easy to explore and explain.
